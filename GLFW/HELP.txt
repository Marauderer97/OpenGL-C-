Controls:

Mouse:
1)Scroll up/down to zoom in and zoom out
2)Right click and drag to pan the scene
3)Left click anywhere on the screen to get the cannon ready, and release to launch a cannonball

Keyboard:
1)'R' to reset the cannonball so it can be launched again if you are in a hurry
2)'UP' to zoom in
3)'DOWN' to zoom out
4)'RIGHT' to pan right
5)'LEFT' to pan left
6)'U' to start controlling the cannon with the keyboard
7)'SPACE' to launch the cannon
8)'S' to decrease the launch power
9)'F' to increase the launch power
10)'A' to increase the launch angle
11)'B' to decrease the launch angle
